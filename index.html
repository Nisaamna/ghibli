Explore Studio Ghibli with Us !

Studio Ghibli, Inc. is an animated film studio based in Koganei, Tokyo, Japan. His anime films contain provocative, imaginative, emotional elements, and have received widespread praise from all over the world.

History

Initially Studio Ghibli was created with the aim of producing animated films whose production was led by Hayao Miyazaki and Isao Takahata. In 1997, Studio Ghibli merged with Tokuma Shoten, but finally became an independent company in 2005.Today, Studio Ghibli has become a strong presence in the animation industry. Studio Ghibli's works are so famous globally, this studio even has a special museum displaying the animated aesthetics of Studio Ghibli films and also themed cafes in various parts of the world with the 'Ghibli' theme.

Movies

Studio Ghibli is very famous for its animated films that provide warmth and coolness to the heart. This success cannot be separated from the handiwork of the legendary animator, Hayao Miyazaki.

Spirited Away

Spirited Away is a historic anime film, not only for Studio Ghibli and Japan, but for the anime world in general. The following is a synopsis of Spirited Away, which won many awards, including an Oscar. Spirited Away tells the magical adventures of a 10 year old girl named Chihiro. The journey began when he was forced to follow his father and mother to move to a new area. On the way, they got lost. The car driven by Chihiro's father enters a mysterious forest and ends up in a building with a tunnel.

Then they found a shop that served hot food. Without permission, Chihiro's father and mother ate the food with the assumption that they would immediately pay for it as soon as the shop owner appeared.

Howl Moving Castle

The film Howl's Moving Castle tells the story of Sophie, who is an 18 year old hat maker who accidentally meets the witch Howl when she is visiting her sister Lettie. When he returned home, he met a witch from the wastes who turned him into a woman in her 90s and couldn't tell anyone about it. Sophie leaves her house and helps a living scarecrow trapped in the bushes, then she takes Sophie to Howl's moving palace. After entering, he met Howl's student Markl and a fire demon named Calcifer. Calcifer is the palace's energy source. Howl returns to his palace and sophie introduces herself as a cleaner hired by calcifer.

My Neighbor Totoro

It is told about a large, hairy creature in the house where the brothers will live. This creature has become iconic and has been the mascot of Studio Ghibli for many years. Hayao Miyazaki is smart enough to present this character, making this film full of intrigue and suspense.

The shape of this character is a combination of an owl (fukuro), neko (cat) and tanuki (raccoon). Some people consider this animated character created by Hayao Miyazaki to be a tree-guarding spirit, some even think of it as genderuwo in Indonesia. Even though this creature is not as scary as Genderuwo.

This creature from Hayao Miyazaki's imagination was created to be a cheerful creature who likes children. Even the two brothers in the film really liked it. This creature often gives gifts to Satsuki and Mei in the form of seeds that can transform into large trees, in fact this character is very cute and adorable.
